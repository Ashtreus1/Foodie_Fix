What's New

Chicken Adobo

Filipino Chicken Adobo recipe has tender, juicy chicken cooked in a tangy, intensely-flavorful sauce that is quick to throw together and delicious served over white fluffy rice.

Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy is the ultimate comfort food! Made with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, it's hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.

Sisig

Sisig is probably the most famous Kapampangan dish ever! Get this easy and authentic Sisig recipe using grilled pork belly! Crunchy and spicy just the way it should be.

Crispy Fried Chicken

Extra crispy, flavorful, and easy to prepare chicken wings that are perfect for a party or social gatherings!

Filipino Style Chicken Curry

Filipino Style Chicken Curry is cooked with coconut milk and this makes it creamier and more flavorful.

Longsilog

This dish features Filipino sweet sausage (longganisa) served with garlic rice and fried eggs. The sweet and savory sausage pairs perfectly with the aromatic garlic rice and the rich, runny yolks of the fried eggs.
