Longsilog

Information

Filipino Longsilog is a popular breakfast meal in the Philippines, featuring longganisa (Filipino sausage), sinangag (garlic fried rice), and itlog (fried egg). It is a delicious and satisfying dish that combines sweet and savory flavors, perfect for starting the day or any mealtime.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces Filipino longganisa
  • Leftover white rice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Vinegar (optional, for dipping)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Longganisa:
    • In a skillet, cook the longganisa according to package instructions until they are browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sinangag:
    • In the same skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until golden brown and fragrant.
    • Add the leftover rice to the skillet. Stir-fry the rice with the garlic, breaking up any clumps, until the rice is heated through and slightly toasted. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Fry the Eggs:
    • In another skillet, fry the eggs sunny-side-up or according to your preference.
  4. Assemble the Longsilog:
    • Arrange the cooked longganisa, sinangag, and fried eggs on a plate.
    • Serve the Longsilog hot, with vinegar on the side for dipping the longganisa. Enjoy this classic Filipino breakfast meal that's bursting with flavor!
