Filipino Crispy Fried Chicken

Information

Filipino Crispy Fried Chicken is a popular dish known for its flavorful and crispy texture. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of garlic, onion, soy sauce, fish sauce, and spices, then coated in a mixture of flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and paprika before being fried to golden perfection. This dish is a favorite in Filipino households and is often served with rice and a side of dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or 1/2 cup milk mixed with 1/2 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, garlic, onion, soy sauce, fish sauce, ground black pepper, and salt. Mix well to coat the chicken evenly. Marinate for at least 1 hour, or overnight in the refrigerator for better flavor.
  2. Prepare the Coating:
    • In a separate bowl, mix the flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and paprika until well combined.
  3. Coat the Chicken:
    • Dip each chicken piece into the buttermilk, then dredge in the flour mixture, making sure to coat the chicken thoroughly. Shake off any excess flour.
