Filipino Chicken Curry

Information

Filipino Chicken Curry is a comforting and flavorful dish made with chicken, vegetables, and a rich curry sauce. It is a fusion of Filipino and Indian cuisine, featuring the aromatic flavors of curry powder, coconut milk, and a variety of spices. This dish is often served with steamed rice and is perfect for family meals or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, minced
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Steamed rice (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Saute the Aromatics:
    • In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Saute the onion, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the Chicken:
    • Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add the Vegetables:
    • Stir in the potatoes, carrots, and bell pepper.
  4. Simmer with Sauce:
    • Pour in the coconut milk, curry powder, fish sauce, and soy sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir to combine.
  5. Simmer and Serve:
    • Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
    • Serve the Filipino Chicken Curry hot over steamed rice. Garnish with chopped green onions. Enjoy the delightful flavors of this comforting and aromatic dish!
